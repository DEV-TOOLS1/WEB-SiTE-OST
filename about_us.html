About OSTPE

Learn more about our mission and the team behind the technology

Our Mission

OSTPE is dedicated to providing professionals and home users with a lightweight, reliable bootable system for computer maintenance, repair, and recovery. We believe that everyone should have access to powerful recovery tools without complexity.

Our Story

2008

The Beginning

OSTPE was created by Adam Viaene with a vision to simplify system recovery and maintenance for everyone. Starting as a personal project, it has grown into a trusted tool for IT professionals and home users worldwide.

2015

Growth & Expansion

As the need for reliable recovery tools increased, OSTPE expanded its toolkit with advanced diagnostics and network support, making it essential for IT professionals everywhere.

2024

Modern Era

OSTPE continues to evolve with modern tools and features, maintaining its core philosophy of simplicity and reliability while embracing new technologies.

2026

Today

With a complete website redesign and renewed commitment to user experience, OSTPE continues to serve the community with cutting-edge recovery and maintenance solutions.
